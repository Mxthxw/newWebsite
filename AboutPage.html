About us

We are a group of enthusiastic gamers who enjoy socializing, playing, and chatting about all things gaming and e-sports

Established

We were established in 2022 in the post covid era when we all finally got to go to university in person. The idea for our organization came about while we were chatting during a game of Valorant. Right now our focus on on growing our gaming community inside our university but hope in the future to arrange tournaments with other gaming organizations at other universities.

What we are

Inclusive and open to everyone who wants to enjoy gaming and e-sports. We want to help facilitate and make friendships around the common interest of gaming.
